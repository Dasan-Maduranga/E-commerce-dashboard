E-commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

This interactive E-commerce Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency to support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard highlights key performance indicators such as total sales amount, unit price totals, delivery time, and customer session duration. It enables analysis of monthly revenue trends, city-wise customer engagement, and product performance. User behavior insights are visualized through pages viewed, customer ratings, device usage, and gender distribution.

Advanced filters allow users to dynamically explore data by time period, device type, city, and product category, making it easier to identify trends, optimize marketing strategies, and improve overall business performance.

Key Insights Covered:

Sales performance and monthly revenue trends

Customer engagement by city and device type

Delivery efficiency and session duration analysis

Demographic insights through gender distribution

Tools & Focus:
Data Visualization, Business Intelligence, Performance Metrics, Actionable Insights (Power BI)
